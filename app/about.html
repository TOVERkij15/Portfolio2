About

I'm Tasha Kijsamnong, designer and entrepreneur. After graduating from Anderson University where I earned a B.S in Liberal Studies, I decided to drop everything and attend The Iron Yard Academy. Here I developed a passion for front-end engineering and a new sense of creativity where I could be challenged every day. Coming from a background in architecture and interior design, I am now able to implement these concepts into building web applications that are not only creatively functional but user-friendly.
